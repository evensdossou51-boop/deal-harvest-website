Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 5, 2025

Thank you for visiting Dealharvest ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit our website, subscribe to our email list, or interact with our content.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information other than your email address, which you voluntarily provide when subscribing to receive deals, offers, or updates.

We do not collect:

  • Payment information
  • Personal identifiers (such as name, address, phone number)
  • Browsing data tied to your identity

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to:

  • Send you updates and promotional offers about new products or deals
  • Provide information related to our website and affiliate partners (e.g., Amazon)

You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any message you receive.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use Google Analytics and Google Ads to help understand website performance and improve our content. These services may collect anonymous data such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Referring websites

This data is collected through cookies and does not personally identify you.

To learn more:

  • Google Analytics Privacy & Terms
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps

You may opt out of Google's advertising personalization using the Ad Settings page.

4. Amazon Associates Program

Dealharvest is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

As an Amazon Associate, we may earn from qualifying purchases made through our affiliate links. Amazon may place cookies to track referrals and purchases. For more information, visit the Amazon Privacy Notice.

5. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • You can request that we stop processing your data at any time.
  • You may withdraw consent for email communications by unsubscribing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at dealharvest26@gmail.com.

6. CCPA (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal data)

To make a CCPA request, contact us at dealharvest26@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your email address only as long as you remain subscribed. Once you unsubscribe, your email is deleted from our mailing list automatically.

8. Data Security

We use secure systems and third-party services to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
